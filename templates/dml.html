We think you're tops, you've made it a long way to the DML Finals, and you deserve a badge to memorialize the achievement! Plus, it gives us a chance to show off our fancy new Issuer API. Click the badge below to send it to your backpack.

What happens when I click on the badge?

When you click on the badge above, a few things happen - you'll be asked to sign in to your backpack, then an assertion will be created that verifies you own the badge. Your backpack will grab the badge image, grab the assertion data, and bake them into a badge that's now hosted on your Mozilla Open Badges backpack.

The process of adding all this fancy issuing stuff is a relatively easy process. As long as you (as an issuer) have a hosted assertion (in simple JSON format) and can add a few lines of javascript, then you'll be able to issue a badge. Easy Peasy.

If you want to dive into the code a bit, start by viewing the source of this page!
